The polishing process was very educational. I learned where various codes belong such as font styles and colors. I enjoyed gaining the knowledge to how to change links and link colors as well. DreamWeaver made the coding easier to understand and showed what attributes could go where. I think it would have been beneficial to have learned using DreamWeaver first because you can see what changes are happening as you are coding, however, my knowledge from handcoding also helped with understanding the many attributes and tags. This project was one of my favorites so far learning how to use Dreamweaver! Some of tha changes I made were changing my tables to percentages, my link colors such as the visited links or rollover links, as well as font styles and italicizing my headings. I look forward to applying what I learned to my final website's design.
