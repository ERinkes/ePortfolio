About Elizabeth Rinkes


 I am in my sixth year of teaching and couldn't be happier with my career choice. My first year I taught third grade in Lorain City Schools. After one year at that position, I moved to my current position at Brunswick City Schools. I am currently a 5th grade teacher at Kidder Elementary. For two years, I taught three sections of Language Arts and one section of Math, however, the past three years it has been Language Arts and Social Studies. I also coach cheerleading at the middle school. My current license include PK-3, 4-5 endorsement, and 4-9 in both Math and Science. I am currently pursuing my Master's Degree in Instructional Technology at The University of Akron that should be conferring in Summer 2019.
