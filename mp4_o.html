For me, the validation process was quite simple. There were not many errors and for some they were careless mistakes. For example, an error that I continued to receive was end tags being omitted. I looked back through the 3 mini projects and found a couple errors of lacking a closing tag or by correcting the direction of the quotation marks. This fixed many of my listed errors. I also found that I coded some in the wrong for order and I simply needed to rearrange the td or tr tags. I did find another error that I spent a lot of time trying to fix and that was my audio tag. Overall, this was a very informative project and I learned a lot with the reasoning behind all the codes!
