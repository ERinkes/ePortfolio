Welcome to Elizabeth Rinkes's MP6: Digital Video

For this project, I had to learn iMovie. I had not used it before, so it was frustrating at first. However, as soon as I got a hang of it, it was extremely easy. I decided to do a video that showcased the activities I did in class or some fun things around our building. I also added photos of my cheerleaders since that is most of my time in the fall and winter. I enjoyed using the various editing tools such as the transition tools and various effects to the photos. I liked how many different captions I could add to the photo as well. It was very easy to stretch the photos or captions so that the timing fit better with the music. I enjoyed working on this project and have ideas on how to implement iMovie into our curriculum for upcoming years.
