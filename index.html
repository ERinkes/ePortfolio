Elizabeth Rinkes's Technology ePortfolio

My name is Elizabeth Rinkes. I am in my fifth year of teaching and Brunswick City Schools. I am currently a 5th grade teacher at Kidder Elementary. We are not self-contained, so I teach only Language Arts and one class of Social Studies. I am lucky enough to see all the fifth graders throughout the day because of this. I also coach cheerleading at the middle school. I received my BS from Trine University in Angola, Indiana and am currently pursuing my Master's Degree in Instructional Technology at The University of Akron.

The purpose of my ePortfolio is to showcase the skills I have developed through my coursework in the Master’s Program of Instructional technology at the University of Akron. Throughout the program, I have explored the National Educational Technology Standards and have mastered how to implement the skills in my teaching. The artifacts on this page are directly aligned to the standards while demonstrating my expertise and application in the field. In addition, this page will provide assistance to colleagues in developing a learning environment conducive to 21st century learners.
